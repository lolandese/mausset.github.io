## Guest Rooms in Agriturismo in Val Pellice

Stanza dei Nonni, Soppalco dei Gatti, Fienile Grosso, Camera di Magna Marianna... here at Mausset each room has its own name, its own personality, its own style.

### Available Services

At the disposal of guests:
- Two equipped kitchens
- Heat pump
- Refrigerator
- TV (in Fienile Grosso)
- Balconies
- Internet connection (in Fienile Grosso)

### Important Information

Reservation a few days in advance is essential, especially during the winter period since the rooms must be adequately heated. Due to this, winter rates are the highest.

The amount is calculated based on the room chosen, not the number of people occupying it.

### Our Rooms

| Room | Description |
|------|------------|
| **Stanza dei Nonni** | Double room with kitchen |
| **Soppalco dei Gatti** | Loft for additional guests or relaxation area |
| **Fienile Grosso** | Space for meetings, courses, dormitory |
| **Magna Marianna** | Double bedroom in agriturismo |
